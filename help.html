Frequently Asked Questions

Getting Started

How do I create an account?

Creating an account is simple! Click the "Login" button in the top navigation, then select "Sign Up" on the login page. Fill in your name, email, and password, and you'll be ready to start using our financial tools.

Is Ghar Ka Guide free to use?

Yes! Our basic financial tools are completely free to use. We offer premium features for advanced analytics and personalized recommendations, but you can access most of our functionality without any cost.

What financial tools are available?

We offer several powerful tools: Budget Optimizer for expense tracking and budget planning, Investment Guide for portfolio management and investment advice, and an AI-powered chatbot for instant financial guidance.

Budget Optimizer

How does the Budget Optimizer work?

Our Budget Optimizer analyzes your income and expenses to provide personalized recommendations. Simply input your monthly income and categorize your expenses, and our AI will suggest optimal spending limits and savings targets.

Can I track multiple income sources?

Absolutely! You can add primary income, secondary income (freelancing, investments), and other income sources. The tool will automatically calculate your total monthly income for budget planning.

How accurate are the budget recommendations?

Our recommendations are based on proven financial principles like the 50/30/20 rule and industry best practices. However, personal circumstances vary, so we encourage you to adjust the suggestions to fit your specific needs.

Investment Guide

What investment options does the guide cover?

Our Investment Guide covers mutual funds, stocks, fixed deposits, gold, bonds, and other popular investment vehicles in India. We provide risk assessment, return projections, and portfolio diversification advice.

How does the risk assessment work?

We evaluate your age, income stability, investment experience, and financial goals to determine your risk tolerance. This helps us recommend appropriate investment strategies and asset allocation.

Can I get personalized investment advice?

Yes! Our AI-powered system provides personalized recommendations based on your profile. For complex financial situations, we recommend consulting with a certified financial advisor.

Account & Login

I forgot my password. How do I reset it?

On the login page, click "Forgot Password?" and enter your email address. We'll send you a secure link to reset your password. Check your spam folder if you don't receive the email within a few minutes.

Can I change my email address?

Yes, you can update your email address in your profile settings. You'll need to verify the new email address before the change takes effect.

How do I delete my account?

To delete your account, go to Profile Settings > Account Management > Delete Account. Please note that this action is irreversible and all your data will be permanently removed.

Technical Support

The website is loading slowly. What should I do?

Try refreshing the page or clearing your browser cache. If the problem persists, check your internet connection or try using a different browser. You can also contact our support team for assistance.

I'm getting an error message. What does it mean?

Error messages usually indicate a temporary issue or invalid input. Try refreshing the page or checking your input data. If the error persists, please contact our support team with the exact error message.

Is my data secure?

Yes, we use industry-standard encryption to protect your data. All information is stored securely and we never share your personal financial data with third parties without your consent.

Billing & Payments

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, UPI, net banking, and digital wallets like Paytm, PhonePe, and Google Pay for premium subscriptions.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. Your premium features will remain active until the end of your current billing period.

Do you offer refunds?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for new subscribers. If you're not satisfied with our premium features, contact our support team within 30 days for a full refund.
