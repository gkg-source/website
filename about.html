About Ghar Ka Guide

Empowering Indian families with smart financial planning tools and expert guidance

Our Mission

At Ghar Ka Guide, we believe that every Indian family deserves access to professional financial planning tools and expert guidance. Our mission is to democratize financial planning by making it simple, accessible, and affordable for everyone.

We understand that managing finances can be overwhelming, especially for families who are just starting their financial journey. That's why we've created an intuitive platform that combines artificial intelligence with proven financial strategies to help you make informed decisions about your money.
